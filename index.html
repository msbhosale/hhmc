About Us

With a blend of experienced educators, dedicated medical professionals

For over 25 years, we have proudly contributed to the field of education, shaping the future of numerous students with dedication and passion. In 2006, we expanded our journey by establishing the Late Mrs. Housabai Jaypal Magdum Public Highschool in Nimshirgaon. Every year, our school continues to nurture a large number of students, empowering them with the knowledge and skills to succeed in life.
